Pug

  1. Pugs are an ancient breed. They used to be treated like royalty.
  2. The perfect pug tail has 2 curls.
  3. Their short noses are a big health issue.
  4. A group of pugs is called a grumble.

Cocker Spaniel

  1. Cockers can be loud, so make sure you train them to know when to be quiet.
  2. Be gentle when training them, as they are soft.
  3. Their big, beautiful ears can be a health problem, be sure to check them regularly.
  4. They are known for their merry temperament.

Golden Retriever

  1. Golden Retrievers love to swim. Take them to a lake regularly.
  2. They are considered the 4th smartest dog breed.
  3. They are known to take care of other animals.
  4. Don't leave your Golden Retriever alone for too long. They are known to become depressed.

Chihuahua

  1. The Chihuahua is the world's smallest dog breed.
  2. People in Mexico and the USA celebrate the Chihuahua on Cinco de Mayo.
  3. Humans and Chihuahuas share the number 1 cause of death: cardiovascular disease.
  4. There are deer headed and apple headed Chihuahuas.

Siberian Husky

  1. Huskies are perfectly equipped for severe winter weather.
  2. Heterocromia (differently coloured eyes) can occur relatively often in Huskies.
  3. They have super-friendly personalities.
  4. Huskies can sprint for hours upon hours without using many of their resources, because of their incredible metabolism.
